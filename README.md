## ğŸ¯ The Problem

> Students often book separate rides or food orders to the same destinations/restaurants, leading to extra cost and wastage.

Our challenge was to build a platform for students to share rides and group food orders easily, with add-ons for group chat, split payments, live tracking, and verified profiles.

## ğŸ’¡ Our Solution

**wheel2gether** is a web-based platform designed for college campuses. It directly connects students who are heading to the same destination or ordering from the same restaurant.

By creating a simple and trusted network, our platform helps students:
* **Save Money:** Split ride fares and food delivery fees.
* **Reduce Waste:** Consolidate multiple orders into one, reducing delivery traffic and packaging.
* **Build Community:** Connect with fellow students in a safe, verified environment.

Our platform successfully implements the core requirements of the problem statement:

* **ğŸ›¡ï¸ Secure Authentication:** Users can create an account and log in. This ensures that only verified students from the community can access the platform.
